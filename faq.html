Custom Builds & Orders

Usually, custom pressing and assembly takes between 7-14 business days depending on our current queue. Intricate grip tape laser cutting may add an additional 2 days to the total time.

Maintenance & Care

It depends on your environment. If you skate in dusty or wet conditions, you may need to clean them monthly and replace them every 6-12 months. Clean environments can stretch bearing life up to 2 years.

Use a specialized grip gum (like a giant eraser) to rub away dust and dirt. For mud, let it dry first, use a soft wire brush to break it up, and then apply the grip gum.

That squeak usually comes from the urethane pivot cups rubbing against the metal hanger of your trucks. A few shavings of paraffin wax dropped into the pivot cup will silence it immediately.

Unfortunately, no. Once moisture seeps into the wood plies, the deck loses its stiffness and "pop" permanently. Always try to keep your board out of rain and puddles.

It's entirely personal preference. However, you should never tighten the kingpin nut so much that it bulges and crushes the bushings. If you need tighter trucks, upgrade to harder (higher durometer) bushings instead.
